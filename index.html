Aim of a Hackathon

  • Similar to an Agile Spike - needs to prove / validate an idea
  • Different contexts / perspectives
  • Demo the idea visual & if possible with a prototype
  • Capture many ideas - even the ones you didnt do + reasons

Teams Tips

  • Must be transparent & work towards the same goal
  • Treat it like a 2 day Agile Sprint
  • Hourly Standup - keep in sync
  • Revalidate / Reflect
  • Always have a working example
  • Pair
  • KIS

Presentations tips

  • Describe the problem, and your solution
  • Explain what challenges you had & what changed
  • Tell a story about your journey & paint a picture
  • People feel they went on the journey with you

Hackathon pitfalls

  • High risk items on the 2nd day - Fail fast
  • Pivoting late - mention possible changes in presentation
  • Timebox everything - Spending too much time on a single item
  • Breadth or Depth - e.g. user journey with low fidelity wireframes or single designed page

Conclusion

  • Hackathons are great: to generate / validate new ideas, build bridges & break down silos
  • Digitise all notes / diagrams etc for after the event
  • Beneficial to the people & the company
  • Make the most of the event, the more you put in the more you will get out
  • If you are not tired by th end, you didnt put in enough
